How We Teach

Lessons & Micro-Lessons:

At QPalX our Lessons is the fundamental unit of work. A Lesson is broken down into multiple Micro-Lessons with progressive degrees of difficulty. Each Micro-Lesson is a custom QPalX short curated video which presents selected sub elements of a Lesson in a fun and engaging presentation allowing Students to learn and grasp concepts quickly. After going through each Micro-Lesson, Students will able to build mastery of all key concepts presented in a Lesson.

Quizzes:

At the end of every Micro-Lesson Students will be assessed to determine their level of understanding around concepts presented in each Micro-Lesson. This is a crucial part of our Teaching Methodology as we are able to gather instant real-time feedback on the Students level of understanding and mastery. How Students perform on these quizzes will directly impact their proficiency ratings that we calculate in the Curriculum.

Question Banks:

Question Banks are randomly selected Take Home problems which Students can use to practice concepts presented in a given lesson offline. Question Banks are not directly scored or impact a Students performance on our platform.
